My Favorite fun and easy Snack!

Yummy Yogurt Parfait

You will need
  • Granola (any kind)
  • Greek Unflavored Yogurt
  • Clover Honey
  • Fresh Fruits

Directions

  1. Drop a tablespoon of honey in a bowl
  2. Pour a cup of yogurt into the bowl
  3. Spread fresh fruit generously over the yogurt
  4. Pour half a cup of granola in the center

Enjoy this whenever you're in the mood for snacking and just want something to hold you up until the next meal! It's delicious and healthy!
